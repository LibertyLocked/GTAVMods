Rainbow Neon
by libertylocked

This mod makes neon kit color on a vehicle dynamic. It creates a rainbow transition on the neon color!
To adjust transition speed, open RainbowNeon.cs and change the TICK_INTERVAL (which is in milliseconds).

Requirements: 
1. ScriptHookV 
http://gtaforums.com/topic/788343-vrel-script-hook-v/
2. ScriptHookV DotNet 
http://gtaforums.com/topic/789907-vrel-community-script-hook-v-net/ 

Installation: 
1. Put "RainbowNeon.cs" in "scripts" folder.
